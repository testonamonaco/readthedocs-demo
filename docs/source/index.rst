Overview of GeoData Collector
===================================

**GDC** is a web-based platform that allows users to manage, analyze, and visualize geospatial data related to agriculture, territory management, water resources, and other related fields. The platform is powered by ESRI's ArcGIS Hub, which provides advanced capabilities for geospatial data management, visualization, and analysis.

With **GDC**, users can upload and manage various types of geospatial data, including raster and vector data, as well as satellite imagery. The platform also allows users to create maps, charts, and graphs to visualize their data and perform advanced geospatial analysis.

In addition to data management and analysis, your GeoHub also supports collaboration among users. Users can share data, maps, and analysis results with other users on the platform, making it an excellent tool for team projects or collaboration with other organizations.

The platform offers a comprehensive set of tools and features that can help users in various stages of data analysis, from data collection to visualization and analysis.

Overall, **GDC** is a powerful tool for anyone working with geospatial data in agriculture, territory management, water resources, and related fields. With its advanced capabilities and user-friendly interface, it can help users make data-driven decisions and collaborate with other experts in their field.
